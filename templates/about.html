About MangoSnap

Bridging the gap between traditional farming and modern Artificial Intelligence.

MangoDetect is a Final Year Project designed to assist farmers and quality control officers in instantly analyzing mango ripeness. By reducing human error and waste, we aim to optimize the harvest supply chain.

👨‍🌾

Information

Built on state-of-the-art Computer Vision architecture.

🧠 Model Architecture

We utilize YOLO11n (You Only Look Once - Nano), optimized for real-time detection on edge devices.

  • ✅ Lightweight: Fast performance even on standard laptops.
  • ✅ High Accuracy: Fine-tuned specifically for mango features.
  • ✅ Real-Time: Processes images in milliseconds.

📊 The Dataset

Robustness comes from data. Our model was trained on a rigorous dataset:

  • 📸 1,300 Training Images collected from diverse environments.
  • 🔄 Data Augmentation applied (Rotation, Zoom, Flip) to prevent overfitting.
  • 🏷️ 3 Classes: Ripe, Unripe, and Overripe.
